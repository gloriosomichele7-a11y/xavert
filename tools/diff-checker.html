About Diff Checker

Diff Checker compares two texts line by line directly in your browser. It highlights changed lines, counts differences and calculates a similarity percentage without uploading your content.

  • Line-by-line text comparison
  • Difference highlighting
  • Character and line statistics
  • Similarity percentage
  • Copy and download results
  • Local browser processing
