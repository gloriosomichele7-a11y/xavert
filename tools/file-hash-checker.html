FAQ

Are my files uploaded?

No. Files are processed entirely inside your browser.

Which algorithms are supported?

SHA-256, SHA-384 and SHA-512.

Can I verify a downloaded file?

Yes. Paste the expected checksum and compare it with the generated hash.
