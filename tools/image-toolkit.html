Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded?

No. All image processing is performed locally in your browser.

Which image formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG and WebP are supported for input and export where applicable.

Does metadata cleaning remove EXIF information?

Yes. Exporting through the browser canvas removes embedded EXIF metadata.
