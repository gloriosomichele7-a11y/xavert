Frequently Asked Questions

Is my JWT uploaded?

No. JWT decoding happens entirely inside your browser.

Does this tool verify the signature?

No. It decodes JWT tokens but does not verify their cryptographic signature.

Can I inspect the header and payload?

Yes. Both sections are decoded and displayed in a readable JSON format.
