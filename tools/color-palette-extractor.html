About Color Palette Extractor

Color Palette Extractor analyzes an image and identifies its dominant colors. It is useful for graphic design, branding, UI design, web development and digital art.

  • Extract dominant colors from any image.
  • Generate HEX, RGB and HSL color values.
  • Copy individual colors or the entire palette.
  • Download palettes as TXT, JSON, CSS or SVG.
  • Everything runs locally in your browser.

Color Palette Extractor FAQ

Here are the most common questions about extracting color palettes online with XAVERT.

  • Which image formats are supported? Any image format supported by your browser, including JPG, PNG, WebP and GIF.
  • Is my image uploaded? No. Images are processed entirely inside your browser.
  • Can I download the palette? Yes. You can export it as TXT, JSON, CSS or SVG.
  • Can I copy individual colors? Yes. Each extracted color can be copied in HEX, RGB or HSL format.
